During my time at Picture, we were tasked to create templates with reusable content modules to aid Music Journalists in the field during major UK music festivals.

These templates were designed to run with the BBC's 'Old Stack.' They were in no way powered by a CMS so markup had to be simple to allow the content teams to make quick edits.

Custom skinning of components was done for each festival.

With the release of the BBC's new stack 'Forge' these sites have been superceded by a far superior event CMS known as Evis.
