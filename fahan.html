Way back in 2010 my good old friend and collegue Adam Hill we asked to give the Fahan School's website a refresh.

We worked closely with the school to develop information architecture and range of templates that supported the needs of returnig parents, teachers and students as well as parents interested in sending their daughters to study at Fahan.

We also developed a custom Rails based CMS.

It's held up really well for a 4 year old site, and we're hoping to give it the responsive treatment soon.
